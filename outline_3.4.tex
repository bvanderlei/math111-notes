The {\bf Chain Rule } for derivatives:
\begin{displaymath}
  \frac{d}{dx}\left[f(g(x))  \right] = \frac{df}{dg}\frac{dg}{dx} \\
  \end{displaymath}
\begin{displaymath}
  \left[f(g(x))  \right]' = f'(g(x))g'(x)\\
  \end{displaymath}
if $f$ and $g$ are differentiable functions.
